The Problem

B2B sales teams waste significant time crafting individual LinkedIn messages that often read as generic templates. The result: low response rates, inconsistent follow-up, and missed pipeline opportunities. Meanwhile, your team's LinkedIn network represents thousands of untapped, high-value relationships.

The Solution

Our LinkedIn Outreach Platform transforms your existing LinkedIn connections into a structured, AI-personalized outreach pipeline. It generates tailored multi-message sequences for each prospect—adapting tone, content, and timing based on the relationship context—and delivers them in a ready-to-send format.

"Instead of one generic template blasted to hundreds of contacts, every prospect receives a sequence that references their specific role, company, and industry—written in seconds, not hours."

Step 1: Import Your LinkedIn Connections

Install our lightweight Chrome extension. With one click from your LinkedIn connections page, it imports your contacts—names, titles, companies, and connection dates—directly into the platform. You can also upload a CSV export from Sales Navigator or any spreadsheet.

Step 2: Organize & Segment Your Prospects

The platform automatically deduplicates contacts and organizes them into a searchable, filterable database. Segment by company, title, industry, or status. Select the exact prospects you want to target for a campaign.

Step 3: Generate Personalized Sequences

Select your prospects and choose an outreach style—Cold, Warm, or Referral—based on your relationship. The AI engine generates a complete 4-message sequence for each contact, personalized to their role, company, and industry. Hundreds of unique sequences are created in minutes.

Step 4: Review & Deploy

Review each generated sequence in the platform. Edit any message to add a personal touch. Then copy and send through LinkedIn at the recommended cadence. The dashboard tracks progress across all your active outreach.

The Outreach Sequence

Each prospect receives a structured 4-touch sequence over 14 days:

Day 0
Connection Request — A concise, personalized message referencing their role and a relevant industry insight. Ends with a qualifying question to spark conversation.
Day 3
Follow-Up #1 — Shares a specific data point or trend relevant to their industry. Positions your team as a thought partner, not a vendor.
Day 7
Follow-Up #2 — References a relevant case study and offers a complimentary assessment. Provides a clear, low-friction next step.
Day 14
Friendly Close — A brief, respectful wrap-up that leaves the door open. No pressure, no false urgency—just a standing invitation to connect.

Three Outreach Styles

Style When to Use Tone
Cold No prior relationship; identified as a target prospect Insight-led, professional, value-first
Warm Already connected on LinkedIn but haven't engaged Conversational, references shared connection history
Referral Introduced or referred by a mutual contact Trust-based, leverages mutual relationship

Getting Started

Implementation is straightforward and requires no engineering resources:

  • Sign in with your Google account at the platform URL
  • Install the Chrome extension (provided as a zip; takes 2 minutes)
  • Navigate to your LinkedIn connections page and click "Scrape to App"
  • Select your target prospects and choose an outreach style
  • Click "Generate" — review your personalized sequences
  • Begin sending on LinkedIn using the recommended cadence

Time to first outreach: Under 15 minutes from login to sending your first personalized sequence.

Security & Access

The platform is hosted on Vercel with enterprise-grade infrastructure. Authentication is handled via Google OAuth—no new passwords to manage. Each user's data is fully isolated.
